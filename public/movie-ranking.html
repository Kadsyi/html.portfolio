Some Movies to Check Out

White Sun of the Desert

This is an adventure film taking place during the Russian Civil War, it depicts a lone wolf revolutionary trying to go back home to his fiancee but runs into people in need on the way every time he turns his course towards his birthplace.


Grave of the Fireflies

Film hits us with the weight of the story right from the beginning with the opening line: main character, a little boy telling us when he died. This is a tragic movie taking place in Japan during the Second World War, not for the faint hearted.


Come and See

Another heart wrenching film set during WW2 but this one takes place in the Nazi occupied Soviet Union. Excellent use of visuals and audio along with brilliant acting passes emotions it wants to convey perfectly. You will be nailed to your seat for a while when the movie ends.
